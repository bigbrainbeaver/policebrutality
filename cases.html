Murder of Mahsa Amini

At september 12th 2022, an Iranian 23 year old college student by the name of Mahsa Amini was arrested for allegedly not wearing the hijab in accordance with government standards. Her brother, who was with her when she was arrested,[37] was told she would be taken to the detention center to undergo a "briefing class" and released an hour later. Her brother was later informed his sister had a heart attack and a brain seizure at the police station to which she had been taken. Later through some scans and medical tests it was obvious that Mahsa was a victim of physical damage, which was caused, obviously, by the deputies themselves.
